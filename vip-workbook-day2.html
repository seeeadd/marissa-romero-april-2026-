Map Your Upsell Funnel

A $47 product alone made $1,566. Adding upsells turned it into $3,978+. Then 3 of those buyers became $10K+ clients. Map your full funnel here.

The Math That Changes Everything

52 sales at $47 = $1,566 (front end only). Add upsell 1 ($97, 7.7% conversion) + upsell 2 ($297, 4% conversion) = $3,978 total. That's 2.5x more revenue from the SAME traffic. Every upsell you skip is money you're leaving on the table.

The premium tier. Community access, live calls, 1:1 element, or your full program.

Downsells (for people who say no)

Each upsell gets a downsell: same product, $20-50 off. "Wait - how about [product] for just [lower price]?"
