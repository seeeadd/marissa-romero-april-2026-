Launch Email Templates

5 emails. 5 days. Use these frameworks and feed them into the AI Email Prompt to generate your actual copy.

Email 1: The Announcement

Subject: It's here (and it's $47)
Angle: Excitement + what they get
CTA: Direct link to sales page
Tone: "I built this for YOU"

Email 2: Social Proof

Subject: [X] people already grabbed this
Angle: Early buyer reactions + FAQ answers
CTA: "Join them"
Tone: Validation + trust

Email 3: Objection Crusher

Subject: "But will this work for me?"
Angle: Address #1 hesitation directly
CTA: "Here's proof it works"
Tone: Empathetic + evidence

Email 4: Deadline + Bonus

Subject: Bonus disappears in 24 hours
Angle: Add a time-limited bonus
CTA: "Grab it before midnight"
Tone: Urgency without pressure

Email 5: Last Chance

Subject: Last call - cart closes tonight
Angle: The 3 options (do nothing / do it yourself / let me help)
CTA: "This is your moment"
Tone: Direct, honest, encouraging
