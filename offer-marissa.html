Not at all. Every step inside assumes you are starting from zero — no course creation experience, no tech skills, no existing audience. The AI prompts and templates do the heavy lifting. Most people in the program had never created a digital product when they started.

YouTube gives you information. The Launch Lab gives you implementation. Inside, you get the specific prompts, the done-for-you templates, the monthly coaching calls, the private community, and a 21-day calendar that tells you exactly what to do each day. Most people inside are doing this around full-time jobs, kids, and busy lives. The 21-day calendar is designed for focused sessions — not 8-hour days. And because you have lifetime access, there's no deadline. Life happens — the program will still be here.
